This is a Classic picadillo recipe for ground beef that is typically eaten over white rice

It can also be used as a filling for tacos or empanadas.

Ingredients

  • 1 table-spoon olive oil
  • 1 small, chopped onion
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
  3. Add ground beef into the skillet
  4. Stir tomato sauce, olives, raisins, capers into the ground beef mixture
