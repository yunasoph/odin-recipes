Pikliz

Pikliz is a Haitian pickled slaw with cabbage, bell peppers, carrots and Scotch bonnet.

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 2 red or yellow bell peppers, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 medium red onion, sliced
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • 1 Jamaican Scotch bonnet pepper
  • 3 tablespoons whole allspice
  1. Stuff cabbage, carrot, bell peppers, onion scallions, Scotch bonnet pepper, allspice, peppercor, cloves and bay leaf into a mason jar.
  2. Add vinegar and lime juice and press down on the ingredients. Seal the jar and shake once.
  3. Refrigerate for 2 days for flavours to meld.
